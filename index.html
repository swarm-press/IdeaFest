What is IdeaFest?

A 5 Catalyst Funds Tradition by SWARM

This Event is fully about Networking and Connecting Proposers to each other, to get insights and to raise awareness about submitted proposals for Project Catalyst Fund 10. We invite you to join us on Ideafest and to present your Fund10 Proposal(s) to the Cardano Community. You will have 3 minutes to pitch and present your Proposal Your presentations will be recorded by our Swarm hosts.. the recordings will be sent out to the Community as a serie of video shorts.

lidonation support

Swarm & Lidonation collaborate to recognise presenters through a Idea Fest Fund 10 Presenter NFT which will be minted through the Lidonation Platform.

Swarm collects receiving addresses, whitelists them and hands over to lidonation. Lidonation users can get their NFT though their Profile on the Lidonation Platform.
